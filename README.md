MilkrFX is a trading technology provider specializing in rule-based trading software, analytical indicators, and structured signal delivery for MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5. Trading signals are generated by external systems, while trade execution and risk management are governed by disciplined, predefined logic within the software. MilkrFX provides trading software and signal services. Future platform extensions, including mobile support, are under evaluation as part of the MilkrFX technology roadmap.
